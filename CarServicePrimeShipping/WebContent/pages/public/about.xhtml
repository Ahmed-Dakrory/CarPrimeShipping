About the Company

Prime International Shipping LLC operates as an ocean freight forwarder (licensed OFF) with a focus on international auto, watercraft, and equipment shipments. We operate four (4) shipping terminals, where cargo is received, loaded into ocean containers AND RO/RO , and shipped to overseas destinations:

  • Savannah (GA)
  • Newark (NJ)
  • Houston (TX)
  • Los Angeles (CA)
  • Indianapolis (IN)

We have well-established relationships with major US auto auctions, such as IAA, Copart, Manheim, Adesa, and others. Through our partnership with USA AutoMarket, we offer our customers auction access and annual membership, with full access privileges to the largest salvage and used car market. We assist with purchase, required documentation, auto parts, and additional services as needed to assure a smooth process and hassle-free delivery of cargo.

We offer real-time cargo tracking so our customers can access information such as photos, condition reports, and up-to-date location of shipment.
